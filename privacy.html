Privacy Policy for TetraVerse

Last updated: May 22, 2026

TetraVerse is an independent Solana dApp project created by Nicolas Juanly Napitupulu for the Solana dApp Store. This policy describes how TetraVerse handles wallet connection data, anonymous leaderboard data, support requests, and data deletion requests.

Developer contact: nicolasjuanlynapitupulu@gmail.com
Support and privacy requests: nicolasjuanlynapitupulu@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains how TetraVerse collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile game and related services. By using TetraVerse, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

TetraVerse may collect limited anonymous gameplay data, including leaderboard scores, game progress indicators, device-related diagnostics, and general usage statistics. This information is used to operate game features, improve performance, detect abuse, and provide a better player experience.

Leaderboard data may be processed and stored through Firebase. This may include an anonymous player identifier, score value, timestamp, and related gameplay metadata needed to operate the leaderboard. We do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information for leaderboard participation unless you voluntarily provide it through support communication.

2. Solana Wallet Connection

TetraVerse uses Solana Mobile Stack (SMS) to enable wallet connection and blockchain-related features. Wallet interactions are handled through compatible Solana wallet software and Solana Mobile Stack standards.

We do not store, request, or have access to your private keys, seed phrases, wallet recovery phrases, or wallet passwords. You are responsible for keeping your wallet credentials secure.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate and maintain gameplay features, including anonymous leaderboard scores.
  • Improve game balance, performance, stability, and user experience.
  • Detect cheating, fraud, abuse, or unauthorized activity.
  • Respond to user support requests when users contact us directly.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Anonymous gameplay and leaderboard data may be processed by trusted third-party service providers such as Firebase, only as needed to operate and improve TetraVerse.

Blockchain transactions may be publicly visible on the Solana network. Public blockchain data is not controlled by TetraVerse and may be independently accessible through blockchain explorers or network infrastructure.

5. User Choices and Data Deletion

You may request deletion of leaderboard data or support-related information associated with you by contacting us at nicolasjuanlynapitupulu@gmail.com. Please include enough information for us to identify the relevant data, such as your player identifier, wallet public address if applicable, approximate score date, and the request you want us to process.

We will review deletion requests and respond within a reasonable period, generally within 30 days where feasible. Some data may be retained when necessary for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, dispute resolution, or to maintain the integrity of the leaderboard.

We cannot delete public blockchain records from the Solana network because blockchain transactions are maintained by decentralized network infrastructure outside TetraVerse's control.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data handled by TetraVerse. However, no digital system can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Users should protect their devices, wallets, and account access credentials.

7. Global Privacy Standards

TetraVerse is designed to respect global privacy standards and principles, including transparency, data minimization, purpose limitation, and user control. Where applicable, we aim to comply with relevant privacy laws and regulatory frameworks such as GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and other international privacy requirements.

8. Children's Privacy

TetraVerse is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children below the minimum age required by applicable law. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can review and remove the information where appropriate.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
